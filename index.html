FAQ

Do you store my Stripe data?

Minimal metadata for run logs and dedupe only. Payout line items go straight from Stripe → your Google Sheet. Tokens are encrypted. You can revoke access anytime.

Do I need Zapier?

No. SyncGreat is a purpose‑built connector—lighter, cheaper, and easier to set up.

What permissions do you request?

Stripe: read‑only for payouts/balance transactions. Google: drive.file and spreadsheets (only files we create or you pick).

What will it cost?

There’s a generous Free tier. Pro is expected at ~£15/mo. Business plans for teams and higher volumes.
